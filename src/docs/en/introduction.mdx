# Introduction

Welcome to the project!  
This application uses **[Kepler.gl](https://kepler.gl/)** to visualize geospatial data directly in your browser.  
With Kepler.gl, you can easily upload datasets, explore interactive maps, and create beautiful visualizations without writing any code.

## Key Features

- 📍 Upload CSV, JSON, or GeoJSON files
- 🗺️ Explore and filter map data interactively
- 🎨 Customize map styles and layers
- 📤 Export maps and share with others

## Who is this for?

This tool is designed for:

- Data analysts
- Researchers
- Anyone working with location-based data

# How to Upload Your Own CSV, JSON, or GeoJSON File to Kepler.gl

Uploading your data to Kepler.gl is quick and easy.

## Steps

1. **Open the application** in your browser.
2. **Click on** the "Layer" button.
3. **Click on** the "Add Data" button.
4. **Choose your file** from your computer.
5. **Wait for the data to load** — the map will automatically update.
6. **Customize** the map layers and styles as needed.
